Who uses Scrollback?

Community Managers

Create an engaging and interactive community using Scrollback. Empower your members to go beyond static comments and nurture interesting, two-way conversations and fruitful discussions.

Bloggers and Website Owners

Build a community around your website and blog by engaging your visitors in real time, immersive conversations.

IRC Channel Operator

Integrate your IRC channel with your Scrollback room and bring it to the wider audience.

Event Managers

Create a buzz around your event with Scrollback. Help your audience connect and network with one another, engage with the speakers and stay involved through extended discussions.
