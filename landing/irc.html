Integrate your IRC channel with your Scrollback room and bring it to the wider audience. Don't lose on those new contributors for whom IRC is not only an unknown concept, but also a tough set up.

Configure your Scrollback room

You can configure your Scrollback room to link it to an existing IRC channel.

Link your Scrollback room to your IRC channel

If you are the channel operator for the IRC channel, type the prompted message on your IRC client to link your Scrollback room to the IRC.
