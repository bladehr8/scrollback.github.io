Scrollback, your portal to IRC

Integrate your IRC channel with your Scrollback room and bring it to the wider audience.

Configure your Scrollback room

Create a chat room on Scrollback. While configuring your chat room, you will find option for IRC setup. It will ask you for two parameters - your IRC server (eg. irc.freenode.in, irc.moznet.in) and the name of your IRC channel (eg. #ubuntu, #python).

Link your Scrollback room to your IRC channel

Ask the ChanOP to send the message /invite 'SecretKey' #ChannelName Shared with you on completion of configuration. Done! The Scrollback bot will join the channel.
