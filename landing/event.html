Scrollback at your event

Improve participant interactions and experiences by helping your audience connect and network with one another.

A communication bridge between spakers and audience

Break the tradition of having 'one-to-many' (speaker-to-audience) communication format at technical events by giving your participant a platform to network more freely, share anecdotes related to the talks and have extended QA with speakers.

A common platform for making announcements

If your conference has parallel tracks or your audience is dispersed in more than one central location, Scrollback can be used as the platform for the event organizers to make all the important announcements.

Build a community around your conference

Most tech conferences end with the end of the event. Building a network of all your participants might help you get better feedbacks and also have your participants return for future events.

Give your speakers a better understanding of the target audience

Give your speaker a chance of communicating with participants before the commencement of the event to help them prepare their content better.
